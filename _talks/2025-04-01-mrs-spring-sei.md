Oral presentation on atomistic molecular dynamics simulations of solid-electrolyte interphase (SEI) formation at the Li metal/Li₇P₃S₁₁ interface using machine-learned interatomic potentials. Results reveal key decomposition pathways and reaction mechanisms governing sulfide solid-state battery interfaces.
